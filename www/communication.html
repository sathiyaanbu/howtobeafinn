Finns don’t talk much.
  There’s no ”awkward silence” for Finn.
There’s just silence.
They can enjoy each others company for hours and not saying a word.  Unless drunk.

But when they are drunk, the can tell you their life story and after that invite you to after party in their sauna.

Forget Sir, Madam, Doctor...
Finns are very informal and usually use first names.

Shake hands for the first time only.
After that it will feel just weird. And no kisses, for heavens sake!

Need to call someone?
There are no public phones in Finland anymore. So you better equip with mobilephone and prepaid.

R-kioski is the place to go to buy prepaids and anything from train tickets to fishing licenses.
