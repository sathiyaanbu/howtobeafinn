Usually Finns don’t talk much to people they don’t know.

But when they are drunk, the can tell you their life story and after that invite you to after party in their sauna.

When Finns are silent they are not being rude. They just don’t consider they have anything worthy to say.

Forget Sir, Madam, Doctor... Finns are very informal and usually use first names.

Finns are quite direct. That doesn’t mean they show disrespect towards someone. That’s just how they are raised. To say what you mean.

Shake hands for the first time only.
After that it will feel just weird. And no kisses, for heavens sake!
