Hi Friend! Welcome to Finland.
This is your ultimate guide how to master the art of being a Finn. Let me walk you through what the game is all about.

How To Be a Finn is an educational game to help learn all the important things about your new home country. Whether you are working, studying, unemployed, refugee, married to a Finn or just passing through this great country, we hope this game will help you.

The game is about learning new things about Finland. But it’s also about activating people to participate in your local community, find new friends, new hobby, maybe a new job?

It has small learning ”classes”, each containing about 5-10 learning cards with some important information about Finland.

Each class will take about 3-5 minutes of your time.   Your progress will be saved automatically so you can continue where you left.

Some classes include tasks that challenge you do something very typical Finnish.    Maybe taste a local food, go to a rock concert, try local sport or sing in a karaoke bar.

So we will tell you the essential things about Finland: whether it’s about the food, culture, people, working in Finland.   We will also tell you where to find the sweetest spots, the hidden gems.
Those places that only the locals know.

And by ”We”, we mean all the other new comers and foreigners that have once been there.
The game is about learning new stuff but also bringing people together and sharing experiences.   So we invite you to play the game, learn and create more content for others.
