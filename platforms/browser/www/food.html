Remember to add spices   Finnish food is generally rather tasteless. So you better add salt and spices.

Porridge for breakfast  .
For breakfast people have usually either porridge (oat, rye) or rye bread. And coffee. Plenty of coffee.

Coffee  !!!
Did you know that Finland is the first at coffee consumption in the world with 9,6 kg per capita, consumption of 2,64 cups/day

Peasoup and pancakes on thursdays  .
During the war the Finnish army was fed with pea soup (hernekeitto).
Finnish Defence Forces as well as many cafeterias continues the tradition of serving pea soup accompanied by pancakes (pannukakku) for lunch every Thursday.

Pizza, pizza, pizza
There are plenty of pizzerias in Finland. Cost per pizza is around 5-10 euros.
You can either choose betweenKotipizza (big Finnish franchise)or one of the small independentpizzerias.
