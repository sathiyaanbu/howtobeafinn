Hi Friend! Welcome to Finland.
This is your ultimate guide how to master the art of being a Finn. Let me walk you through what the game is all about.

How To Be a Finn is to help the user to explore Finland and it's culture whether you are working, studying, unemployed, refugee, married to a Finn or just passing through this great country, we hope this game will help you.

It has small learning ”classes”, each containing about 8 learning cards with some important information about Finland.
Each class will take about 2-4 minutes of your time.

It’s also about activating people to participate in your local community, find new friends, new hobby, maybe a new job?.

Some classes include tasks that challenge you to do something very typical Finnish. Maybe taste a local food, go to a rock concert, try local sport or sing in a karaoke bar.

We will tell you where to find the sweetest spots, the hidden gems.
Those places that only the locals know. So we invite you to play the game, learn and create more content for others.

Finland is safe – so relax.
You have landed probably one of the safest places in the world.   

There are no dangerous animals.
The streets are quite, hardly any pollution, food and water is
clean.

Punkki!
Well actually there is one thing to aware.
The most dangerous animal is the tick (”Punkki”).
Small, blood sucking insect that carries for example the Lyme disease.

It appears during the summer and usually lives at the ground in the vegetation.
You should wear long pants while walking in the forest and longer grass.
Make sure to check your body at evening in case it has bitten.

But during winter your safe.
How lucky we are to have whole land frozen half the year!

Finns might enjoy silence but most of them really like to help foreigners in need.
Finns talk surprisingly good English.
So don’t hesitate to ask for help.

Yep, It's freezing out there. Or wet.
Probably both During winter the temperature might go low as -30C.
The key is to wear clothes in many layers
(in Finnish: ’kerrospukeutuminen’).

So wearing 3 pants on top of each other is just OK.
Remember to buy good beanie or woolly hat.
